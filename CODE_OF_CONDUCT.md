# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

This project follows respectful academic and engineering collaboration standards. Contributors are expected to:

- Communicate with care and professionalism.
- Give constructive feedback that focuses on the work.
- Credit original ideas, datasets, notebooks, and references.
- Avoid harassment, exclusionary language, or personal attacks.
- Raise concerns early when results, methods, or claims are unclear.

## Academic Integrity

This repository supports a Singapore Polytechnic coursework project. Contributions should preserve attribution, avoid plagiarism, and clearly separate original notebook content from later engineering improvements.

## Reporting

If behaviour in this project becomes unsafe or inappropriate, contact the repository owner or the relevant academic supervisor. Reports should include the context, links or files involved, and the requested follow-up where possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate these standards. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation in the repository.
